Research

I am a PhD student at Inria supervised by Marc Lelarge and Guillaume Baudart. I work with the Picube team at IRIF and the Argo team at Inria Paris. My research investigates the reasoning capabilities of Large Language Models. Concretely, my thesis focuses on improving their ability to write proofs and programs in the proof assistant Rocq. Beyond my thesis, I am interested in lambda calculus and type theory, topics at the foundations of Rocq.
